Acknowledgments

The design and markup is entirely replicated from distill. After several renditions, I have landed this approach. Previously, I used Sphinx. The two main reasons I tried using Sphinx was that I was able to render jupyter notebooks and I had hoped the raw markup of .rst would be more natural to read and write. However, it ended up being both overly constricting and underwhelming at the same time.

I use Adobe XD CC for diagrams, D3 for visualizations, and PyTorch and Torch (Lua) for implementations.
